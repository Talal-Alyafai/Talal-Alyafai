## About Me

I'm a **Data Scientist and AI Engineer** with a strong foundation in full-stack development. I focus on building end-to-end intelligent systems — from raw data pipelines to production-ready AI applications. I care deeply about clean architecture, security-conscious design, and systems that actually work in the real world.

- Specialized in **Machine Learning, LLMs, and Data Engineering**
- Building **secure, offline-first AI systems** with a focus on practical deployment
- Strong in both **research-oriented** ML work and **production backend** engineering
- Based in **Oman 🇴🇲**, working across AI, telecom, and enterprise domains
